About the Loan Waiver Program

Learn about eligibility criteria and benefits

Eligibility

Farmers with outstanding agricultural loans, small business owners affected by economic downturn, and citizens with educational loans may qualify.

Benefits

Relief from loan repayment burdens, reduced interest rates, extended payment periods, and complete waiver in qualifying cases.

Requirements

Aadhaar card, loan documentation, income proof, and supporting evidence of financial hardship are required for application.
